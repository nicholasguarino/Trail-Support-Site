Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Trail?

Trail is a number guessing game where you need to find a path of numbers that sums up to a target number. You can move up, down, left, or right on a 4x4 grid to create your path. You have 5 attempts to guess the correct path.

What do the colors mean?

After each guess, the tiles will be colored to help you:

  • Green: Correct number in correct position
  • Yellow: Correct number in wrong position
  • Red: Number not in the path

How do I know if I'm close to the target sum?

After each guess, you'll see an arrow indicating if your sum is higher or lower than the target number. Use this feedback to adjust your next guess!
